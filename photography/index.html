I found an old DSLR camera one year ago, and I've taken it with me wherever I've gone since. Capturing the picturesque beauty of the world around me through photos has broadened my appreciation for nature and aesthetics. Like art, photography is a journey. The pursuit of a distinct, developed style is an endless one. I use a Canon EOS Rebel T3i to take photos. I hope you like them! P.S. Click on the photos to see them in full resolution.
