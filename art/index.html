I love to draw in my free time. The art you’ll find here covers my journey encompassing various inspirations, mediums, and tools. For digital art, I either use my Lenovo IdeaPad, the Lenovo Pen 2, and Sketchbook or, as seen in more recent art, my 9th gen iPad, the Apple Pencil, and Procreate. Most of my traditional art is watercolor on construction paper.

Time goes fast when I put a pencil on paper or a screen. Art is so fun to me because there’s always room to improve. For the past two years, my art has revolved around fictional characters as I’ve tried to develop my style. You might notice that a lot of my work is unfinished or uncolored. My inspiration is quite untempered and fickle, so I lose interest in pieces rather quickly. Even so, I hope you like them!
